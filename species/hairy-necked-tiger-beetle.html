Hairy-necked Tiger Beetle

Cicindela hirticollis rhodensis

Hairy-necked Tiger Beetle (photo: your name / date or iNaturalist observer)

Distribution map (based on iNaturalist observations)

Identification

  • Medium-sized (11–14 mm), bronze with dense hair on thorax ("hairy neck").
  • White maculations variable; legs long.
  • Found on sandy beaches and river shores.

Habitat in New Brunswick

Sandy beaches, riverbanks, and coastal dunes. Prefers open, sandy areas near water.

Flight Season in NB

May to August (peak June-July).

Distribution & Notes

Primarily coastal and riverine in NB. Sensitive to habitat loss from development and erosion control.
