Cow Path Tiger Beetle

Cicindela purpurea purpurea

Cow Path Tiger Beetle (photo: your name / date or iNaturalist observer)

Distribution map (based on iNaturalist observations)

Identification

  • Medium-sized (12–15 mm), bright purple-red to green with white maculations.
  • Body metallic; fast on bare ground.
  • Early spring species; short activity period.

Habitat in New Brunswick

Bare ground paths, roadsides, and open fields. Prefers dry, sunny areas with minimal vegetation.

Flight Season in NB

April to June (early spring; fall rare).

Distribution & Notes

Scattered in southern NB; early-season specialist. Often seen on dirt paths ("cow paths").
