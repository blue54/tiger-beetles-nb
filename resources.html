Cow Path Tiger Beetle (Cicindela purpurea purpurea) – Additional Information

Known in New Brunswick from only one confirmed record (Reggie Webster, July 2020). Sightings are extremely rare, so any new observations would be valuable!

  • 10000 Things of the PNW – Cow Path Tiger Beetle
    Good overview with photos and natural history information.
  • Montana Field Guide – Cow Path Tiger Beetle
    Status, habitat description, and identification notes (useful for regional comparison).
